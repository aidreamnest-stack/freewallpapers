Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to A Dream Art. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

In short: We collect minimal data, don't sell your information, and use industry-standard security measures to protect your privacy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Any other information you choose to provide in your message

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you newsletters and updates (only if you've subscribed)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize user experience
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security
  • Comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences (like theme selection)
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Improve website functionality

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling cookies may limit some website functionality.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect, monitor, and analyze user data to improve our services. These include:

  • Analytics Services: To understand how users interact with our website
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): To deliver wallpapers quickly and efficiently
  • Image Hosting Services: To store and serve wallpaper images

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they use your information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@adreamart.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
